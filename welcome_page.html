Welcome to DATA602: Principles of Data Science

About This Course

DATA602 provides a comprehensive introduction to the core principles of data science. This course covers essential topics such as statistical analysis, data processing, and machine learning techniques. As part of this course, we will explore various methods to extract actionable insights from complex datasets and understand the impact of data science in different industries.

Why Data Science?

Data science is a rapidly evolving field that combines statistics, computer science, and domain knowledge to solve real-world problems. Understanding the principles of data science is crucial for making data-driven decisions, optimizing operations, and driving innovation. This course aims to build a strong foundation in these principles to help students apply them effectively in their future careers.

Course Objectives

  • Gain a deep understanding of data analysis and visualization techniques.
  • Learn to apply statistical methods to real-world data.
  • Explore machine learning algorithms and their applications.
  • Develop skills to work with large datasets and make data-driven decisions.

Resources and Projects

Throughout the course, students will have access to various resources, including textbooks, online tutorials, and project examples. These resources will help reinforce the concepts learned and provide practical experience in applying data science techniques.
